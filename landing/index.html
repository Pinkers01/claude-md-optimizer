The problem

A CLAUDE.md is alive, and it bloats.

Every Claude Code session adds a rule. Preferences, anti patterns, fixes. After six months your file hits 70,000 characters.

Above 40k you feel it. The agent thinks slower, forgets recent rules, finds two contradicting instructions and picks the wrong one.

01 · DRIFT

Slow agent

Past 40k chars context retention drops. Tasks take longer, burn more tokens.

02 · CONFLICT

Silent conflicts

Stripe vs Mollie, autonomy vs always ask, em dash policy, lite vs full mode. Contradictions stack up.

03 · NOISE

Duplicates

The same rule lands three times. Early sessions, repeats during incidents, copies without cleanup.
